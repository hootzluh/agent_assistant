Get Assistance

[What to Do When You're Not Sure What to Do]

When you run into an issue that you aren't quite sure how to resolve or where to get started,
there are three steps you should take to find the right resolution.
These steps MUST be followed in this order.

Step 1: Use Your Resources

You have several resources at your disposal that you can use to find the answers you need to assist callers.
This is the FIRST step you should take when you need assistance.

Step 2: Ask for Help

Once you have exhausted your resources, if you still are unsure how to assist with the caller's issue,
the next thing for you to do is ask for help.
This is the SECOND step you should take when you need assistance.

Step 3: Request an Escalation

You have several resources at your disposal that you can use to find the answers you need to assist callers.
This is the THIRD step you should take when you need assistance.

When Your Tools Aren't Working

If you are having issues with Teams, or Logging Into Grupo, or have MFA issues or a problem with any of your work tools - don't panic.
There's a fix for that, and you'll find it here.
